About Regal Tees

Founded in 2010, Regal Tees has grown from a small print shop to a leading manufacturer and wholesaler of premium quality t-shirts.

We pride ourselves on using only the finest fabrics and printing techniques to create t-shirts that look great and stand the test of time. Our team of designers and printing experts work closely with both retail customers and wholesale partners to deliver exceptional products.
